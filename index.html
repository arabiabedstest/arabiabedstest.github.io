About Us

Revolutionizing Hotel Distribution in the Middle East and Beyond

ArabiaBeds is the Bedbank division of Almatar Group, created to deliver better hotel distribution across Saudi Arabia, the Gulf, and key global markets. We offer smart tech and local expertise to help hotels and buyers connect, grow, and succeed.

Our Purpose:

To empower hotels with easy-to-use tools, direct connectivity, and profitable partnerships.

Key Benefits:

  • Based in Saudi Arabia, built for the region
  • Direct contracts across KSA, GCC & global destinations
  • 24/7 multilingual support
  • Full control over pricing, availability, and performance

Our Vision:

To become the most trusted hotel distribution platform in the region, connecting global travel demand with local supply.

Our Mission:

To enable scalable and transparent hotel distribution for MENA hospitality using technology and trust.
